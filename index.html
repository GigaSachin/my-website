Our Philosophy

Crafted at the
intersection of art
and astronomy

In 2018, master horologist Élise Marchetti gazed at the night sky above her Geneva atelier and asked a singular question: what if a watch face could hold not just time, but a specific moment in the cosmos?

Every Luminary timepiece begins with a date, a time, and a place. Using our proprietary celestial mapping process, we render the precise positions of stars, planets, and constellations as they appeared at that exact instant — your wedding night, a child's first breath, a summit reached at dawn.

The movement beneath is a hand-assembled Swiss caliber requiring 340 hours of craftsmanship. The result is not merely a watch. It is an heirloom of time itself.

The Journey

From moment to
masterpiece

I
The Consultation

You share your moment — a date, a time, a place. Our celestial architects begin the stellar computation, plotting every visible body in the sky as it appeared at that precise instant.

II
The Map

Our astronomers generate a certified celestial chart. This becomes the blueprint for your dial — each star, each constellation drawn to within an arc-second of accuracy.

III
The Craft

Master engravers spend up to 80 hours etching your celestial map by hand into the dial. The movement is assembled over 12 weeks by a single watchmaker who signs every component.

IV
The Ceremony

Delivery is a private ceremony at our Geneva atelier, where Élise herself presents your timepiece alongside its framed celestial certificate and a leather-bound provenance book.
